Robotics Team Project Problem Solving IEEE ENP

ROBOKO is an intensive robotics competition organized by the IEEE Student Branch at National Polytechnic School of Algiers. This challenging competition brought together talented engineering students to design, build, and program autonomous robots capable of completing complex tasks within strict time constraints.

Our team worked collaboratively to overcome numerous engineering challenges, from mechanical design and sensor integration to algorithm development and real-time decision making. The competition tested not only our technical skills but also our ability to work effectively under pressure and adapt to unexpected obstacles.

  • Implemented advanced sensor fusion algorithms for precise navigation
  • Developed custom control systems for efficient task completion
  • Successfully collaborated with a multidisciplinary team of engineers
  • Demonstrated strong problem-solving skills under competitive pressure
  • Applied theoretical knowledge to practical real-world robotics challenges
  • Programmed the robot using Arduino for sensor processing and motor control
